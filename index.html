About Me


My name is Gabriela Guayara, and I am an aspiring software engineer originally from Ecuador, now living in New York City. After moving to NYC three years ago, I decided to pursue my dream career in computer science. I am currently studying at Borough of Manhattan Community College, where I will complete my Associate's degree in Science.

Through my coursework, projects, internships, and leadership activities, I have developed strong technical skills in languages like Python, C++, JavaScript, and frameworks like React. I have hands-on experience building full-stack web applications using tools like MySQL, AWS, and REST APIs. I also have design and collaboration abilities using Figma, Excel, Slack, and more.


My professional experience includes serving as a career mentor at BMCC, helping individuals with disabilities in my role at Innovative Resources for Independence, and completing technical internships at companies like Nearabl and BlackFacts.com. I am passionate about continuing to gain new skills and experiences that will allow me to become a software engineer. Outside academics, I am actively involved in leadership programs like Moody's Corporation Mentorship and the National Society of Leadership and Success.
